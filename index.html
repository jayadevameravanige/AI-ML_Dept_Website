About the Department

Established in 2020, the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We aim to impart quality education to students and produce skilled engineers who can meet the demands of industry and society. Our curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in core concepts of AI/ML, along with hands-on experience through state-of-the-art labs and industry-linked projects. We foster a vibrant learning environment that encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Vision, Mission & Objectives

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning education and research, producing globally competent professionals who are innovative, ethical, and socially responsible.

Mission

To provide a strong theoretical and practical foundation, foster research and innovation, and establish strong collaborations with industry.

Objectives

To produce graduates who can design intelligent systems, promote ethical AI practices, and pursue higher studies and research.

Programs and Courses

B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

A 4-year undergraduate program focusing on the core principles of AI, data structures, algorithms, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and more.

M.Tech in AI and Data Science

A 2-year postgraduate program for advanced learning in specialized areas like reinforcement learning, computer vision, and big data analytics.

Ph.D. Program

Doctoral program for cutting-edge research in various domains of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Learning Resources

State-of-the-Art Labs

Well-equipped labs with high-performance computing resources, GPUs, and the latest software for practical learning.

Digital Library Access

Access to leading journals like IEEE, ACM, Springer, and a vast collection of e-books on AI and related fields.

Industry-Standard Software

Hands-on experience with tools like TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, and cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud.

E-Learning Platforms

Collaborations with platforms like Coursera and NPTEL to provide supplementary learning opportunities for students.
